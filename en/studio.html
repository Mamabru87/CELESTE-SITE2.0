NATURA.LUCE.ARIA.

A botanical project founded by

Marco Maria Lombardi.

The studio was born as a research

into form, colour and matter,

where nature traverses space.

Each creation has the weight of a sculpture

and the lightness of a breath:

a clear vision, capable of alternating

the ethereal to a firm, material presence.

No physical place, a nomadic model.

C É L E S T E operates exclusively

on commission: a ritual of

botanical beauty for the place.

Services:

Vase en Résidence:

Dialogue between vase, space and flowers.

An élite service where

the composition is conceived

to inhabit the client's space.

Art Direction:

Botanical stagings.

Subscriptions:

Beauty on a fixed schedule.
