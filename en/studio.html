CÉLESTE Floral Studio Milan — Manifesto

Manifesto | Bio

NATURE.LIGHT.AIR.

A botanical project founded by

Marco Maria Lombardi.

The studio was born as an exploration

of form, color, and matter,

where nature moves through space.

Each creation carries the weight of a sculpture

and the lightness of a breath.

A sharp vision, capable of alternating

between the ethereal and a bold,

textural presence.

At CÉLESTE we follow time,

we do not force it. We choose only

seasonal flowers because beauty only

makes sense when it is free. Now.

With no physical location in Milan,

CÉLESTE adopts a nomadic model,

operating exclusively on commission.

A ritual of botanical beauty,

tailored specifically for the space

it inhabits: Vase en résidence.

Services:

Vase en résidence:

A dialogue between the vessel,

the space, and the flowers.

An élite service where

the composition is conceived

to inhabit the client's space.

Art Direction:

Botanical stagings.

Subscriptions:

Beauty on a fixed schedule.
